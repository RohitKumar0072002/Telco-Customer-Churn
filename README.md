## Telco Customer Churn Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge for telecom companies as it directly impacts revenue and customer growth.
This project analyzes telecom customer data to identify key factors influencing customer churn and revenue loss.

Using Power BI, the dashboard highlights patterns in customer behavior, contract types, tenure, and service usage that contribute to churn.

---

## Business Problem

Telecom companies often struggle with:

- High customer churn rates
- Revenue loss due to short-term customers leaving
- Lack of insights into churn drivers

This dashboard helps answer critical questions about why customers churn and how businesses can reduce it.

---

## Dashboard Metrics

Key KPIs analyzed in the dashboard:

- Total Customers: 7043
- Total Monthly Revenue (MRR): ₹456K
- Overall Churn Rate: 26.54%
- Revenue Lost due to Churn: ₹139K

---

## Key Insights

- Overall churn rate stands at 26.54%, indicating a significant portion of customers leaving the service.
- Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn, compared to long-term contracts.
- Customers in the first 12 months of tenure are most likely to churn.
- Lack of technical support strongly correlates with higher churn rates.
- The majority of revenue loss comes from short-term contract customers.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to shift from month-to-month contracts to long-term plans through discounts or bundled offers.
- Improve technical support availability to reduce churn risk.
- Focus retention campaigns on new customers in the first 12 months.
- Introduce loyalty incentives for long-tenure customers.
